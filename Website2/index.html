EcoBeans allows you to use small donations from your locally owned participating coffee shops to put towards a charity of your choice.

Simply install the EcoBeans App on Google Play or the App Store, create an account and your on your way to making the world a cleaner place.

There are 5 easy steps to getting on your way with EcoBeans

  • Install the EcoBeans App on your mobile phone
  • Register with your email and password
  • Purchase a coffee from a participating coffee store
  • Using the app scan the qr code
  • Now choose the charity to donation from your scanned coffee cup
  • Watch you donation contributions grow over time
